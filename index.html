The Dunman Residences Location

The Dakota MRT Station, connected to the Circle Lines subway network, is about 1 minute away from the Dunman Residences. The list of nearby amenities can be found here at The Dunman Residences Location Map.

Gardens by the Bay is only four stations distant from the city center of Singapore. The Dunman Residence is located in the heart of Singapore, with easy access to the Pan Island Expressway (PIE), East Coast Parkway (ECP), and Kallang Paya Lebar Expressway (KPE) for private vehicles.

It is also close to several primary and secondary schools, including Broadwick, Kong Hwa, Choon Cheng, and others. This strategic location is easily accessible and handy for all of your requirements. The Dunman Residences location is ideal for a staycation.
